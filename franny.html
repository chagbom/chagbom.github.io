FRanny

Take a day off!

Pitch

An app that allows users to leverage their networks to find temporary childcare

Problem

Now that caregiving services have gone online, finding a sitter is easier than ever. However, finding a new sitter requires trust-building, which takes time. What do parents do when there is an urgent need for a sitter and their regular care is not available?

Solution

Leverage your FRanny network

We are much more comfortable leaving our children with people we know, or people we know of through people we know

Market

Parent with a somewhat urgent need for a sitter

Monetization

  • targeted advertising
  • retailer kickbacks

Value Add

  • Quickly identify a potential Franny – problem solved
  • Conveniently thank and “pay” sitters via linked retailers (Paying loved ones is awkward)
  • Consolidate and give immediate access to emergency contact info, PCP, first aid resources, allergy or medical dosing information, child likes/dislikes, house rules, as well as an in-app messaging service that allows real-time reminders and updates
